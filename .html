Interesting Facts About Michigan

  • Michigan is the only state in the United States that consists of two peninsulas.
  • It is known as the "Great Lakes State" because it is surrounded by four of the five Great Lakes.
  • Michigan is famous for its automotive industry, with Detroit being known as the "Motor City".
